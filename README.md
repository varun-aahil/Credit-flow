# CreditFlow - MSME Loans

**CreditFlow** is a digital lending platform designed to provide quick, accessible loans to Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs). Our platform offers an easy-to-use interface for businesses to apply for loans, calculate EMIs, and check eligibility for various financial products tailored to MSME needs.

## Overview
CreditFlow aims to provide MSMEs with fast, hassle-free access to loans with minimal documentation and competitive interest rates. The platform allows businesses to:
- Apply for loans with flexible repayment options.
- Calculate loan EMIs and total payable amounts.
- Check eligibility for different loan products.
- Track loan applications and get status updates.

### Key Features:
- **Quick Loan Application**: Simple and easy loan application process.
- **Loan Calculator**: Calculate loan EMI, tenure, and total repayment amount.
- **Eligibility Check**: Instant eligibility check based on business performance.
- **Flexible Loan Terms**: Loans with flexible amounts, tenures, and competitive interest rates.
- **Transparent Criteria**: Clear, simple eligibility criteria based on annual turnover, business age, and credit score.
